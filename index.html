About NSS

National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government initiative that develops students personality through community service.

“NSS ek aisa platform hai jahan hum society ki service karte hue khud bhi develop hote hain".

Activities

  • 🧹 Cleanliness Drive – area clean rakhna
  • 🌱 Tree Plantation – ped lagana aur environment protect karna
  • 🩸 Blood Donation Camp – logon ki jaan bachane me help
  • 🚦 Traffic Control Help – police ko assist karna
  • 🏥 Health Awareness – hygiene, health ke bare me awareness

Benefits

  • 🧠 Personality development – confidence badhta hai
  • 🗣️ Leadership skills – team handle karna seekhte ho
  • 🎓 Certificate milta hai – future me kaam aata hai
  • 🤝 Social connection – naye log aur network banta hai
  • 🏕️ Camps & experience – new experiences milte hain

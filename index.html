About NSS

Service Before Self

National Service Scheme

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. It encourages students to participate in community service, leadership development, social awareness and nation building.

Helping society through volunteer work.
